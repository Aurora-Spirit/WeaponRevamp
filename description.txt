This mod aims to rework the functionality of every vanilla weapon.
Tack this onto your modded playthrough to help freshen up the experience, and make vanilla weapons new and interesting again!
This mod also adds a couple new weapons to fill gaps in progression.
This is a beta version. Not every feature is finished yet.

Current features:
-51 weapons reworked
-1 weapon added from old gen console, reworked as well
-2 new ammos (1 arrow and 1 bullet)

Planned features:
-Most weapons reworked (there are a couple weapons that were changed in 1.4.4 that are not planned to be reworked)
-All old gen console weapons added and reworked
-Many new weapons (27 are planned so far)
-5 new darts, to keep your dart gun viable until moonlord for you dart gun fans
-A mod config system to let you manually enable or disable individual reworks for your perfect playthrough, in case you hate my changes for your favorite weapon

Report bugs, provide feedback, and suggest ideas to me on discord: @aurora_spirit

I'm a solo dev, so updates will likely take a while. Be patient!
